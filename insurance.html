Health Insurance Programs in India

Explore government initiatives for affordable healthcare:

  • Ayushman Bharat - PMJAY: Free healthcare for economically disadvantaged citizens.
  • Employees' State Insurance Scheme (ESIC): Medical benefits for workers.
  • National Health Mission (NHM): Rural healthcare accessibility programs.
  • Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana (RSBY): Insurance coverage for low-income families.
